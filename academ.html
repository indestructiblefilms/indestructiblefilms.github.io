INDESTRUCTIBLE FILMS ACADEMY

INDESTRUCTIBLE FILMS Academy Provides free, in-depth industry training to young people interested in a career in media, film or TV.

From absolute beginners, through to industry standard, we support young people to develop the skills, and confidence they need to be career ready. Opening up creative opportunities to learn from professionals, grow a network and most importantly access life changing opportunities and get paid work.

Online and workshop Masterclasses

Open to 16-38 year olds
If you want to discover more about your favourite INDESTRUCTIBLE FILMS and what happens behind the scenes on set, watch on or join the conversation.
Actors, film professionals and our team in training share experiences and answer questions via Zoom, Insta Live,Live Stream and workshop on the INDESTRUCTIBLE FILMS.
